Recipe for pasta carbonara with a twist

1.Choose the best spaguetti pasta you find in the supermarket
2.Boil the pasta until al dente
3.In the meantime fry the pancetta, onions, garlic and habanero chili
4.Mix 2 eggs with grated parmesan cheese
5.Drain the pasta and mix with the fried veggies.
6.Add the mix of egg and cheese and move around.
7.Enjoy!
